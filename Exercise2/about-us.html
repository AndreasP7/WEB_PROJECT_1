ABOUT US

Creators

This project was created by two AUEB Students in the final year of their informatics course, Andreas Papakitsos and Dimitris Vosnakis. Both driven by their high passion for computer science, software and website developement, chose to create an e-commerce website to polish their coding skills.

Dimitris Vosnakis

Dimitris Vosnakis is a 22 year old CS Undergraduate. He has previously worked at EY as an intern software engineer. Currently, he is completing his final year of his course while working part time as a delivery driver.

Andreas Papakitsos

Andreas Papakitsos is a 21 year old CS Undergaduate. Born and raised on Athens, he is in his final academic year and has previously worked for a short period at IT support.

Location,Date and backstory of implementation

The idea and its implementation took place in Athens, Greece during the winter semester of the 2023-24 academic year for the purposes of the "Website developement" course. The most visited types of site after social media are e-commerce sites. This combined with the requirements of the project, was the reason the form of the project was chosen.
